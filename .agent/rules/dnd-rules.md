# D&D 2024 Rules Mandate
When conferring about, implementing, or referencing Dungeons & Dragons rules, mechanics, or content:
1.  **ALWAYS** use the **2024** version of the rules (2024 Player's Handbook, 2024 Dungeon Master's Guide, 2024 Monster Manual).
2.  **DO NOT** use 2014 rules unless explicitly asked for a legacy comparison (and even then, prioritize 2024).
3.  Ensure all monster stats, XP budgets, conditions, and spells align with the 2024 revision.
